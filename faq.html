FAQ

Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

A: Just connect to one of the school's printers and you should be just fine.

Q: How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

A: With the school's printer scan your document and then send it to your laptop.

Q: What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

A: Stay at home and let your teachers know so that they can live stream the lesson for you.

Q: How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

A: You can book a room via the HZ Selfservice Portal.

Q: What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

A: You can park your car in the HZ parking lot.
